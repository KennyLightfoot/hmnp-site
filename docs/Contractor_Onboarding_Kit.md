# Contractor Onboarding Kit

Provide this kit to every contracting notary. Collect signatures and acknowledgements digitally.

## 1. Required Documents
- Valid government-issued ID.
- Texas Notary Commission (expires ≥6 months from onboarding).
- Proof of $100k Errors & Omissions insurance.
- NNA background check certificate (issued within past 12 months).
- W-9 form.
- Direct deposit details (ACH).

## 2. Equipment Standards
- Mobile scanner or high-resolution scanning app.
- Laptop/tablet with webcam for RON (minimum 1080p).
- Reliable mobile hotspot or LTE backup.
- Embosser and inked seal.
- Secure document bag.

## 3. Platform Access
- Web app credentials (role: `CONTRACTOR`).
- Proof.com agent account setup.
- GHL contractor portal (availability updates + communications).

## 4. Training Modules
1. SOP v2 orientation (intake → QA chain).
2. Field gear walkthrough and safety.
3. RON session workflow and contingency handling.
4. Dispatch communications & SLA expectations.
5. Payment, travel tiers, and urgency bonuses.

## 5. Policies & Acknowledgements
- Code of Conduct.
- Incident reporting requirements (24-hour notice).
- Acceptable travel limits and safety guidelines.
- Data privacy and secure handling of client documents.
- Agreement to follow SOP change notifications.

## 6. Availability & Coverage
- Submit weekly availability via contractor portal by Friday 5 PM CT.
- Indicate max travel radius and preferred service types.
- Update availability immediately when changes occur.

## 7. Payout & Disputes
- Weekly payouts via ACH (Friday).
- Access payout ledger through `/contractor/payouts`.
- Submit disputes within 5 business days with supporting documentation.

## 8. Support & Escalations
- Dispatch hotline: `832-555-0192`.
- QA desk email: `qa@houstonmobilenotarypros.com`.
- Emergency escalation: owner-operator line.

Contractors must sign the acknowledgement form and complete all modules before accepting assignments.
# HMNP Contractor Onboarding Kit (SOP v2)

**Goal:** Equip new mobile notary and RON contractors to operate under HMNP SOP v2 within 7 days. This kit is used by owner, dispatch, and QA teams to standardize onboarding, compliance, and readiness checks.

---

## 1. Prerequisites & Documentation

| Requirement | Details | Verification Method |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Commission | Active Texas Notary Public commission | Copy of commission certificate |
| Insurance | Errors & Omissions ≥ $100,000 | Policy declaration page |
| Background Check | NNA or approved provider within last 12 months | Certificate upload |
| Training | NNA certification (Loan Signing for LSA track) | Certificate upload |
| Identification | Government-issued photo ID | Secure upload |
| Tax Documents | W-9 on file | Signed PDF |
| Banking | ACH details for payouts | Stripe Connect onboarding |
| Equipment | Mobile scanner/app, laptop/tablet, smartphone, reliable vehicle | Self-attestation checklist |
| RON (if applicable) | Proof.com certification, approved device setup | Platform confirmation |

All paperwork collected via secure Intake Portal (`/contractors/onboarding`).

---

## 2. Onboarding Timeline (7-Day Target)

| Day | Owner Tasks | Contractor Tasks | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Day 0 | Send welcome packet + portal access | Submit documentation | Auto-reminder after 24 hours |
| Day 1 | Verify credentials; create accounts (GHL, Slack, Proof.com) | Complete compliance quiz | Owner signs MSP agreement |
| Day 2 | Schedule orientation call (30 min) | Attend orientation; review SOP v2 | Cover dispatch flow, QA expectations |
| Day 3 | Assign LMS modules: mobile SOP, RON SOP, QA checklist | Complete LMS modules; pass quizzes (>85%) | Failure triggers retake |
| Day 4 | Provide sandbox assignments | Complete two mock scenarios (mobile +/or RON) | QA reviews within 24 hours |
| Day 5 | Review QA feedback; finalize rate card | Sign contractor agreement + rate card | Upload signed documents |
| Day 6 | Activate in dispatch roster; add to coverage map | Install required apps (GHL, Slack, Stripe) | Conduct tech check |
| Day 7 | Schedule first supervised assignment | Shadow/mentored job | QA monitors live; collect feedback |

## 3. Training Assets

- **SOP Documents:** `[docs/SOP_v2.md](SOP_v2.md)`, `[docs/SOP_v2_Dispatch_Procedures.md](SOP_v2_Dispatch_Procedures.md)`, `[docs/SOP_v2_QA_Checklists.md](SOP_v2_QA_Checklists.md)`

  - Field Kit Setup & Safety
  - RON Platform Walkthrough (Proof.com)
  - QA Expectations & Closeout Form
- **Assessments:** LMS quizzes per module + final knowledge check (20 questions)

---

## 4. Coverage & Availability Setup

1. Collect contractor home ZIP and preferred service radius (max 30 miles).
2. Tag skills (`standard`, `loan-signing`, `ron`, `hospital`).
3. Enter availability blocks into dispatch calendar (min 2 weeks rolling schedule).
4. Assign to coverage clusters:
   - Cluster A: 77590–77592 (Texas City/Galveston)
   - Cluster B: 77573/77539 (League City/Dickinson)
   - Cluster C: 77058/77059 (Clear Lake)
5. Set expected response SLA (under 10 minutes for new assignments).

---

## 5. Communication & Tools

- **Slack:** `#field-support`, `#dispatch-alerts`, `#qa-feedback`
- **GHL Mobile App:** receive assignments, update status, upload documents
- **Proof.com:** RON sessions, credential checks
- **Google Workspace:** shared calendars, document repository
- **Support Channels:** dispatch hotline, owner escalation number, QA office hours

Provide quick reference card with links, phone numbers, escalation order.

---

## 6. Performance Expectations

- Client review average ≥ 4.7 with minimum 10 reviews over first 90 days
- QA compliance ≥ 95%
- Punctuality: arrive/check-in within 5 minutes of scheduled time
- Documentation submitted within 60 minutes of appointment end (RON immediate)
- Incident reports filed within 1 hour of issue

---

## 7. Compensation Overview

- Base rates aligned with SOP pricing (see `[docs/SOP_v2.md](SOP_v2.md)` §7)
- Travel fee share (70% to contractor)
- Urgency bonus for same-day or after-hours acceptance
- Witness coordination stipend ($15) + 50% of witness fee when sourced by contractor
- Weekly payouts via Stripe Connect; ledger visibility every Friday

Contractors sign acknowledgment of payout policy during onboarding.

---

## 8. Ongoing Compliance & Continuing Education

- Quarterly refresher webinars (required)
- Annual background check renewal reminder 45 days in advance
- Mandatory updates when SOP version increments (contractors sign change log)
- Random ride-alongs/spot audits for mobile services

---

## 9. Exit & Offboarding

- 14-day notice required unless breach of contract
- Collect HMNP-issued equipment or credentials within 24 hours
- Retain records for 7 years; revoke all system access via access checklist
- Process final payout within 5 business days after outstanding tasks resolved
