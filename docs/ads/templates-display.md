Title: Display Ad Templates

Use: Emphasize very short headlines + clear sublines. Keep claims accurate.

Short Headlines (3–5 words)
- Notary Today, Book Now
- Mobile or 24/7 Online
- Fast, Friendly Notaries
- Transparent Pricing
- Houston Notary Pros
- Same‑Day Appointments

Sublines (≤ 45 chars)
- Mobile to you in {{geo}}
- 24/7 RON available
- Flexible rescheduling
- Book in minutes
- Clear, upfront pricing

Long Headline (optional)
- Book a professional notary in {{geo}} — Mobile to you or 24/7 online.

CTAs
- Book Now • Start Online • Call Now

Creative/do’s
- High-contrast CTA; clear service imagery (documents, signing, webcam)
- Show “Mobile” vs “Online” options distinctly
- Keep copy minimal; push details to landing page

Creative/don’ts
- Don’t imply legal advice or guaranteed outcomes
- Avoid tiny disclaimers; keep claims simple and true
