**Question 2:**
> How much does your mobile notary service cost?

**Answer:**
> Our standard mobile notary service starts at $75, which includes the travel fee for up to 20 miles and the state-regulated fee for the first notarial act. After-hours and weekend appointments start at $125. For a transparent, instant quote, please visit our booking page: `https://houstonmobilenotarypros.com/booking`

---

**Question 3:**
> Are you available on weekends or after hours?

**Answer:**
> Yes, absolutely! We specialize in providing after-hours and weekend mobile notary services for urgent needs. We understand that flexibility is important, and we are here to help when other notaries are closed.

---

**Question 4:**
> Can you notarize documents online?

**Answer:**
> Yes, we offer secure Remote Online Notarization (RON). This allows you to get your documents notarized from anywhere using a computer or smartphone with a webcam. The cost is $25 per notarial act. It's a fast and convenient option for many document types.

---

**Question 5:**
> What do I need to have ready for my appointment?

**Answer:**
> Please ensure all signers are present and have a valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID, such as a driver's license, state ID card, or passport. Also, please have the documents ready but do not sign them beforehand, as you must sign in the presence of the notary.

---

**Question 6:**
> How quickly can a notary get to me?

**Answer:**
> We offer same-day service and can often arrive within a few hours, depending on your location and our schedule. Our online booking system shows the earliest available arrival window for your ZIP code. For the fastest response, call or text us at `(832) 617-4285`.

---

**Question 7:**
> What types of documents do you notarize?

**Answer:**
> We can notarize a wide variety of documents, including Powers of Attorney, real estate deeds, affidavits, medical consent forms, vehicle title transfers (VIN verifications), and I-9 employment verification forms. We are also certified to handle complete loan signing packages.

---

**Question 8:**
> Do you handle loan signings for real estate?

**Answer:**
> Yes, we have certified Loan Signing Agents who specialize in real estate closings. We work with title companies, lenders, and real estate agents to ensure all documents are signed and notarized accurately and professionally for a smooth transaction.
