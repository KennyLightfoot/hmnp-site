### 1. Add Appointment Link

**Why:** Direct booking link increases conversions and improves ranking signals.

**Steps:**
1. In GBP dashboard, click **"Edit profile"** (pencil icon)
2. Scroll to **"Business information"** section
3. Find **"Appointment links"** or **"Booking links"**
4. Click **"Add appointment link"** or **"+"**
5. Enter:
   - **URL:** `https://houstonmobilenotarypros.com/booking`
   - **Label:** "Book Online" or "Schedule Appointment"
   - **Type:** Select "Online booking" or "Website"
6. Click **"Save"** or **"Done"**

**Verification:** The link should appear on your GBP profile immediately (may take a few hours to show in search results).

### 2. Enable Messaging

**Why:** Messaging increases engagement and provides ranking signals. Customers can contact you directly from Maps.

**Steps:**
1. In GBP dashboard, click **"Messages"** tab (left sidebar)
2. Click **"Turn on messaging"** or toggle the switch
3. Set up auto-replies:
   - Click **"Auto-replies"** or **"Settings"**
   - Enable **"After-hours auto-reply"**

**Verification:** Test by sending yourself a message from Google Maps (search for your business and click "Message").

### 3. Verify Business Type

**Why:** Service-area businesses need correct categorization for local search.

**Steps:**
1. Click **"Edit profile"** ‚Üí **"Business information"**
2. Find **"Business type"** or **"Location type"**
3. Verify it's set to **"Service-area business"** (not "Storefront")

6. Click **"Save"**

**Verification:** Your address should not be visible in search results (only service areas).

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Appointment Link Not Appearing
- **Issue:** Link not showing on profile
- **Solution:** 
  - Verify link is HTTPS
  - Check link is accessible (test in browser)
  - Wait 24-48 hours for indexing
  - Contact GBP support if still not appearing

### Photos Not Uploading
- **Issue:** Photos fail to upload
- **Solution:**
  - Check file size (max 5MB per photo)
  - Verify image format (JPG, PNG)
  - Try different browser
  - Clear browser cache

### Q&A Not Showing
- **Issue:** Q&A items not appearing
- **Solution:**
  - Wait 24-48 hours for Google to review
  - Ensure answers don't contain spam/keywords
  - Try re-adding with shorter answers

### Categories Not Updating
- **Issue:** Secondary categories not showing
- **Solution:**
  - Wait 24-48 hours (Google indexing delay)
  - Some categories require manual verification
  - Try removing and re-adding
  - Contact GBP support if needed
