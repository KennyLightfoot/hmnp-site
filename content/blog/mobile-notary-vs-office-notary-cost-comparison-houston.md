Let's compare actual scenarios to see the true costs:

**Office Notary Route:**
- Notary fee: $10
- Your time: 2 hours (travel + waiting)
- Gas/parking: $8
- **Total Cost:** $18 + 2 hours

**Mobile Notary Route:**
- Service fee: $75
- Your time: 15 minutes
- **Total Cost:** $75 + 15 minutes

**Analysis:** Mobile costs $57 more but saves 1 hour 45 minutes. If your time is worth $30/hour, mobile actually saves you $25.

- **Total Cost:** $92 + 8 hours of family time

**Mobile Notary Route:**

- Standard service: $75

- **Total Cost:** $95 + 30 minutes

**Analysis:** Mobile costs $3 more but saves 7.5 hours and eliminates coordination headaches.

### **Scenario 3: Urgent Real Estate Documents**

**Office Notary Route:**

- **Risk:** Deal falls through

**Mobile Notary Route:**
- Same-day service: $100 + $25 = $125
