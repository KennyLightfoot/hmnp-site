# What Documents Need Notarization in Texas? Complete List 2024

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What documents absolutely must be notarized in Texas?
Real estate deeds, mortgages, wills, power of attorney documents, and affidavits must be notarized to be legally valid.

### Can I notarize documents online in Texas?
Yes, Texas allows Remote Online Notarization (RON) for most documents that can be notarized in person.

### Do I need witnesses in addition to notarization?
Some documents (like wills and medical POAs) require witnesses in addition to notarization. Check your specific document requirements.

### How long is a notarized document valid?
Notarization doesn't expire, but the document itself may have an expiration date or validity period specified in the document.

### Can I notarize my own documents?
No, you cannot notarize documents in which you have a financial interest or are a party to the transaction.
